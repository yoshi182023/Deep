RAG bridges knowledge gaps. Retrieval-Augmented Generation lets LLMs access domain-specific information that wasn't in their training data, making them useful for niche or recent content.
RAG 弥补知识空白。检索增强生成让大型语言模型能够访问其训练数据中没有的领域特定信息，使其对小众或最新内容也有用。

Vector databases enable semantic search. Converting text to embeddings allows finding conceptually similar content rather than just keyword matches, dramatically improving retrieval quality.
向量数据库支持语义搜索。将文本转换为嵌入可以找到概念上相似的内容，而不仅仅是关键词匹配，从而显著提高检索质量。

Evaluation is the bottleneck. Without expensive human review, measuring RAG system performance becomes the limiting factor for iterative improvement.
评估是瓶颈。没有昂贵的人工评审，衡量 RAG 系统性能就成为迭代改进的限制因素。

Data recency matters. Newsletter content represents time-sensitive information that highlights LLMs' knowledge cutoff limitations and RAG's value proposition.
数据时效性很重要。新闻通讯内容代表了时间敏感信息，凸显了大型语言模型知识截止点的局限性和 RAG 的价值主张。
